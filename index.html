PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple peanut butter and Jelly sandwich

Ingrediants

  • 2 Slice of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly(grape Jelly)

Instructions

  • Lay out two slices of bread.
  • Spread peanut butter on the slice
  • spread jelly on other two slice
  • Put the two sliuces together
